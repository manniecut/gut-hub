  • Simple Salad

    edit

    by: username

    This is a super easy and tasty salad.

      Ingredients

      1 Cucumbers

      Lettuce

      1 Tomato

      1/2 Onion

      1 Tbsp Olive Oil

      1 Tbsp Vinegar

      Salt

    1. Cut all your veggies up to whatever size you like

    2. Mix them all up in a bowl

    3. Add the oil and Vinegar

    4. Salt to preference and enjoy!
